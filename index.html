Muzyka

Stanislav Szpineta explores a wide variety of sounds, from melancholic melodies to vibrant, rhythmic compositions. His music carries emotional depth, a unique sound reflecting his personal experiences and moods. His debut album, "Echoes of a Fading Mind", released independently, has been praised for its experimental approach and soul-touching rhythms.

Poezja

„Nocna Melancholia” ~Ewa Edyta Zdziera

Poezja Stanisława Szpinety to wrażliwy zapis świata emocji, uczuć i wewnętrznych przeżyć. Oto niektóre z jego dzieł z tomiku "Lawenda":

  • Sam: gdzieś gdzie drga droga ma
  • W wspólnych snach: kwitnąca, świt w nocach
  • Staś: tak mały gdy gra, gdy truje do dna

Filmy

Stanislav's passion for visual storytelling is evident in his short films and video projects. Influenced by VHS aesthetics, his films carry a nostalgic, raw feel that brings viewers back to simpler times. His short film "Memories in Static" was critically acclaimed for its innovative use of vintage equipment.

Archive

The Archive is where Stanislav stores and displays his creative soul’s artifacts: hand-drawn sketches, vintage video clips, and moments captured in analog form. This section represents his deep connection to the past, curating a blend of nostalgia and timelessness in his work.
